# Mail Disclosure Policy

If you're reading this, you've clicked on this link at the bottom header of our e-mail that was sent. When we send e-mail to intended recipients, we need to make sure that you are one of the intended recipients that we needed to disclose information to, and that confidential info exchanged between involved parties won't exit your system to the public.

The information disclosed in e-mail exchanges is naturally confidential, unless this e-mail was posted to publicly-archived mailing lists, at which confidentiality doesn't matter, as long as no private info exists, such as blueprints for our projects.

***

## <mark style="color:$primary;">What should I do?</mark>

If you've encountered an unexpected mail coming from Aptivi or any of our e-mails containing confidential information not meant for you, we recommend that you delete all copies of this e-mail from all your systems immediately to avoid information leakage.

Please do:

* **Delete** all copies of this e-mail as mentioned above from all your mail clients.
* **Delete** all copies of this e-mail from your mail server (if you have one).

Please do not:

* **Open** or **read** this e-mail, as it contains confidential information.
* **Attempt** to get information about confidential information.
* **Send us** e-mails asking us questions or giving us feedback about confidential information.
* **Send** screenshots or video captures about this e-mail, as it contains confidential information.

This policy exists to make sure that confidential information stays protected from the wrong hands, besides the intended recipients and us during our exchange. Always double-check the `To`, the `Carbon Copy (CC)`, and the `Blind Carbon Copy (BCC)` fields to make sure that no wrong mail addresses exist, and to make sure that the mail gets sent to the intended and correct recipients during the delivery of the mail.

If you came here from the mailing lists that are publicly archived, there's nothing you need to do, but if you feel that it contains confidential information, please send them an e-mail requesting the deletion of the information, if possible. Similarly, if we feel that we've disclosed too much, inform us about it.
