# Welcome!

Aptivi was established as an internet community on 2014 when we started providing the&#x20;best solutions and repairs to our fellow computer users. Our years of work have always helped users with the&#x20;most complicated problems, while providing fresh news about our projects and about tech in general.

Aptivi Manual is an official documentation website for our projects and for our other docs. This is to give users a clear guidance of how to use our projects.
